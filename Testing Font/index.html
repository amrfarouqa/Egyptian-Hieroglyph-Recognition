    Egyptian Hieroglyphic Recognition: Transliteration Technique

    In ancient Egypt the formal writing system used is the hieroglyphic system. The Egyptian Hieroglyphs grouped alphabetic elements, syllabic and logographic with a total of more than 900 distinct characters.
